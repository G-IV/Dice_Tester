Interpretation Notes

  • The primary fairness metric is chi-square goodness-of-fit against a uniform d6.
  • TVD is a direct measure of how far the observed distribution moves away from 16.67% per face.
  • Group rankings here measure pooled balance only. They do not establish physical independence between dice.
  • Bias cancellation matters: a sixth-ranked die can improve a five-die pool if its face biases offset the others.
